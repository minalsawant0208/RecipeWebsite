CHOWMIN RECIPE

Ingrediants

200g of chow mein noodles (can substitute with spaghetti) 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil 2 cloves of garlic, minced 1 onion, thinly sliced 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 carrot, julienned 1 cup of cabbage, thinly sliced 2 tablespoons of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce (optional) Salt and pepper to taste Optional toppings: sliced green onions, sesame seeds

Recipe

Cook chow mein noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and sliced onions. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Add sliced bell pepper, julienned carrot, and sliced cabbage to the skillet. Stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes until vegetables are slightly tender. Add cooked noodles to the skillet along with soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using). Toss everything together until well combined and heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, garnished with sliced green onions and sesame seeds if desired.

Tips

Make sure to stir-fry the vegetables quickly over high heat to retain their crunchiness. You can customize the vegetables based on your preference or what you have on hand.
