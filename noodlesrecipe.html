NOODLES RECIPE

Ingrediants

200g Chinese noodles (egg noodles or rice noodles), 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 2 cloves garlic, minced, 1-inch piece ginger, grated, 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, broccoli), 200g protein (chicken, beef, tofu), Salt and pepper to taste, Optional garnishes: chopped green onions, sesame seeds

Recipe

Cook noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside. In a wok or large skillet, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Add garlic and ginger, cook until fragrant. Add protein, cook until browned, then add mixed vegetables and cook until tender. Stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce, then add cooked noodles. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, garnished with green onions and sesame seeds if desired.

Tips

Don't overcook the noodles; they should be al dente. Customize with your favorite vegetables and protein. Adjust seasoning to your taste preference.
