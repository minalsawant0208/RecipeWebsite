PASTA RECIPE

Ingrediants

8 ounces (225g) spaghetti pasta, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 2 cloves garlic, minced, 1 can (14 ounces/400g) crushed tomatoes, 1 teaspoon dried basil, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, Salt and pepper to taste, Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving), Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish),

Recipe

Cook the spaghetti pasta according to the package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving about 1/2 cup of the pasta cooking water. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and add the dried basil and oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine. Let the sauce simmer gently for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly. Once the sauce is ready, add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, you can add a little bit of the reserved pasta cooking water to loosen it up. Serve the spaghetti with tomato sauce hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.

Tips

Use good quality pasta for the best results. Look for brands made with durum wheat semolina for authentic taste and texture. Don't overcook the pasta. It should be cooked al dente, meaning it should still have a slight bite to it.
