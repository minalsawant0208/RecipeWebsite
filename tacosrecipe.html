TACOS RECIPE

Ingrediants

1 lb (450g) ground beef or chicken, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 small onion, finely chopped, 2 cloves garlic, minced, 1 tablespoon chili powder, 1 teaspoon ground cumin, 1/2 teaspoon paprika, Salt and pepper to taste, 8 small corn or flour tortillas, Optional toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, diced avocado, salsa, sour cream, chopped cilantro, lime wedges

Recipe

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and saut√© until translucent, about 2-3 minutes. Add ground beef or chicken to the skillet. Break it up with a spatula and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Stir in chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Warm the tortillas: You can do this by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for about 30 seconds, or by heating them directly on a dry skillet for about 30 seconds on each side. Assemble the tacos: Spoon the meat mixture onto each tortilla. Add your desired toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, diced avocado, salsa, sour cream, chopped cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.

Tips

Use lean ground beef or chicken for a healthier option. Customize the seasoning to your taste. You can add more or less chili powder depending on how spicy you like it.
